About Us

Origin Story

Our humble story begins in a small village in Thailand in 1992. There, young Chef Jav-a-Script nearly burnt down his entire village when he left some rice on the stove and forgot about it as he played Mortal Kombat in his room. While he was able to beat the game and was crowned champion, he was mortified that he had put his village in such danger. From that day forward, Chef Jav-a-Script vowed to perfect his culinary skills, and honestly went a little overboard with it. Today, he lets no dish leave his kitchen that has not gotten his seal of approval, which is hard to do because he has four restaurants. You will not find one single grain of burned rice in your meal.
